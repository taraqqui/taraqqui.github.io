Our Mission

We are working towards educational and social upliftment of rural residents. In addition to education, we are working on the good health of the public around us. Last but not least, we are also working towards environmental safety.

Education

Our educational mission is split in to three prongs. The first is to motivate our children towards education. The second is to spread academic drive throuhgout the village by local physics and mathematics competitions.

Health

In addition to eudcational awareness, another core component of our society's mission is promoting the best practices for maintaining public health. In this recent coronavirus criss, our NGO has made it a top priority to produce ration kits for those who are unable to make a wage during India's lockdown.

Environmental Safety Programs

The final core component of our mission is to promote a cleaner India. We have held rallies and demonstrations with a goal of spreading awareness of the importance of recycling and reducing pollution.

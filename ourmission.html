Our Mission

We are working towards educational and social upliftment of rural residents. In addition to education, we are working on the good health of the public around us. Last but not least, we are also working towards environmental safety.

Education

Taleem-O-Taraqqui Welfare Society is based in the rural part of the country. It is a well known fact that rural India has a very low literacy rate. As a result, we are widely focused on educational awareness in the rural areas. Our educational mission is divided into three main segments: motivational programs, locally organized academic/sport competitions, and career counselling. Our motivational programs center around speeches that we give to students to emphasize the importance of curiosity and learning. In the coming months (delayed due to Covid-19), we plan to host competitions that recognize students who excel in math, English, and general knowledge. The goal of these competitions is to spark a desire to learn in students of all different skill levels. Finally, our career counselling centers around helping students prepare for college entrance examinations and job interviews.

Health

Another core component of our NGO's mission is to promote the best practices for maintaing good health through health awareness campaigns. We will also be conducting sports competitions to promote physical exercises. In the recent coronavirus crisis, our NGO has been distributing ration kits to daily wage labourers on top priority during the complete lockdown in India.

Environmental Safety Programs

Last but not least, under the Indian Government's campaign 'Swacch Bharat Abhiyan', our NGO is also promoting a cleaner India. We have held rallies for spreading awarenss to save water, promote cleanliness, etc. We have also conducted plantation campaigns towards a greener India.

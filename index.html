Our Mission

We are Taleem-O-Taraqqui Welfare Society, a registered NGO who's goal is to increase widespread access to quality information. Through competitions, speakers, and books we hope to spread awareness about the importance of education and the importance of a healthy lifestyle.

What We Do

Being established in a rural zone with a low literacy rate, we are emphasizing the following issues:

  • Motivating Our Children Towards Education

    We motivate our young generation towards education through lectures by achievers around the area. We also host local math and physics competition to spur academic achievement

  • Spreading Health Awareness

    We conduct regular meetings with locals around us to make them aware about a healthy lifestyle. We also conduct free health check-up camps for all throughout the village.

  • Environmental Safety Programs

    As a human being, it's our duty to protect the environment. We teach the people a bout importance of fresh air/water through various campaigns.

Our Coronavirus Relief Effort

33 comments

Distributing Coronavirus Items

Our NGO has been spreading awareness through social media, loudspeaker announcements throughout the village of Nahal, and phone calls about precautions related to the novel coronavirus. Due to the nationwide lockdown, most of the families of daily wage labourers started facing food scarcity, especially in rural areas. Our NGO made a firm plan to provide ration kits to such families a priority. Our team successfully delivered these kits (door to door) to more than 1000 people in nearby places.

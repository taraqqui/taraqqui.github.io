What's Upcoming

Here are a few major upcoming plans of our NGO:

The Library

A top priority of ours. We are planning on setting up a quiet space in Nahal village for students to study, so that students can have lesser distractions and shall be able to concentrate on their studies. Since it is a bigger project for us, it will take a few months to finalize details as well as gather the necessary financial support to accomplish this. We are currently in the process of selecting a suitable place for the library and our target is to make it functional in 2021.

Compilation of Biographies of Achievers

To inspire and motivate our young generation, we are looking to compile a book comprising biographies of Indian Achievers like Dr BR Ambedkar, Mahatma Gandhi, Dr APJ Abdul Kalam, Sir Seyyed Ahmed Khan, Mauulana Abul Kalam Azad etc.

Interschool Competitions for General Knowledge/Mathematics/English

Another platform to motivate young studnets to study hard by conducting various competitions amongst them related to General Knowledge, Mathematics, and English. We will be recognizing the winning students with prizes and certificates.

Blood Donation Camp

In collaboration with a blood bank, we will be organizing a blood donation camp in the near future.

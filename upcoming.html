What's Upcoming

Since we are new, up and coming organization, we have many new events and projects planned in our future. More specifically, we are focused on setting up a small library in the Nahal Village. We are also planning on writing and publishing a book on some of the great achievers of India.

The Library

In the village of Nahal, we are planning on building a quiet space for children to work and study. We hope that by providing such a space, children have more opportunities to learn with less distractions surrounding them. We have currently narrowed down a few possible locations that are accessible to all within the village. We are currently targeting a date sometime in 2021 to begin construction of the library.

Educational Books

In addition to providing the necessary resources for students, another one of our goals is to inspire students to motivate them academically. An upcoming project we are working on is a book about the great achievers of India which we hope will inspire students to learn more and spark their curiosity.
